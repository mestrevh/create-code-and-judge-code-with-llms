Descrição do Problema (problem.txt)

Título: Water Supply Collapse Topicos do problema: decisão; Tempo limite de execução: 1 segundo(s) Descrição:

Recently, Marvel city experienced a water supply collapse.
The transformers that pumped the water exploded, causing enormous inconvenience to the population.
When the utility managed to replace the transformers, scaled, the water supply had to back to normal.
For this, a rotation of supply was carried out in the first week, dividing the city by areas:
  • Area 1: Moon Knight, Daredevil, Jessica Jones
  • Area 2: WandaVision, Loki, Hawkeye
The rotation followed the following scale:
  • Area 1 - water available from 8 am to 8 pm on Tuesday;
  • Area 2 - water available from 2 am on Wednesday;


Formato da entrada:

Neighborhood name

Day of the week

Formato da saída:

Water Available or Water not Available
