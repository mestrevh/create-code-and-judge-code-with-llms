ALDOUS HUXLEY WAS A HUMANIST AND PACIFIST, AND HE WAS LATTERLY INTERESTED
IN SPIRITUAL SUBJECTS SUCH AS PARAPSYCHOLOGY AND PHILOSOPHICAL MYSTICISM.
[1] HE IS ALSO WELL KNOWN FOR ADVOCATING AND TAKING PSYCHEDELICS.
BY THE END OF HIS LIFE HUXLEY WAS CONSIDERED, IN SOME ACADEMIC CIRCLES,
A LEADER OF MODERN THOUGHT AND AN INTELLECTUAL OF THE HIGHEST RANK,
 AND HIGHLY REGARDED AS ONE OF THE MOST PROMINENT EXPLORERS OF VISUAL
 COMMUNICATION AND SIGHT-RELATED THEORIES AS WELL.[2]
